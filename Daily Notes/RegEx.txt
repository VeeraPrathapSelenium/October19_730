Regular Expression:

regular expressions are used to validate or to extract data based on a pattern

pattern is sequence of characters which is created by the user that which maps to the data that is looking for


Generally, we may come across to validate the email id, date of birth etc

when we wantto perofrm such data formats using string method is little costly and leads into performance issues also


To overcome these problems regex are used.

inthis regex , one must be very strong in creating patterns
